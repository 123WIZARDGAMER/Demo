Scuba Diving Programs

Choose the dive that suits you best

Boat Dive vs Shore Dive – What’s the Difference?

If you are new to scuba diving, choosing between a Boat Dive and a Shore Dive can be confusing. Here’s a simple explanation to help you decide 👇

🚤 Boat Dive

In a Boat Dive, you travel by boat to deeper diving locations with rich coral reefs and colorful marine life. This dive offers better visibility and a more adventurous experience.

  • Deeper dive locations
  • More corals & fish variety
  • Best visibility
  • Ideal for adventure seekers

₹ / person

🏖️ Shore Dive

In a Shore Dive, you enter the water directly from the beach. It is done in shallow water and is perfect for first-time divers who want a relaxed and comfortable experience.

  • Shallow & calm waters
  • Perfect for beginners
  • No boat travel
  • Easy & comfortable dive

₹ / person

💡 Our Recommendation

👉 First-time diver? Choose Shore Dive
👉 Want more adventure & marine life? Choose Boat Dive

Not sure? WhatsApp us and we’ll guide you personally 😊

Kayaking Programs

Choose the kayaking experience that suits you

Day, Sunset & Night Kayaking – What’s the Difference?

Kayaking is a calm and beautiful way to explore the waters of Andaman. Each time of day offers a different experience. Here’s a simple guide to help you choose the best one for you 👇

☀️ Day Kayaking

Day Kayaking is done in bright daylight, allowing you to enjoy clear views of the water, mangroves, and surroundings. It is relaxing and easy for everyone.

  • Clear visibility
  • Calm & safe experience
  • Best for families & beginners
  • Great for photos

₹ / person

🌅 Sunset Kayaking

Sunset Kayaking lets you paddle during golden hour, when the sky changes colors. It’s peaceful, romantic, and one of the most popular experiences.

  • Beautiful sunset views
  • Romantic & peaceful
  • Perfect for couples
  • Best time for scenic photos

₹ / person

🌌 Night Kayaking

Night Kayaking is a unique adventure done after dark. With glowing lights under your kayak, you experience the water in a magical and exciting way.

  • Unique night experience
  • LED-lit kayaks
  • Adventure & thrill
  • Guided & safe

₹ / person

💡 Our Recommendation

👉 First time kayaking? Choose Day Kayaking
👉 Couples & photography? Choose Sunset Kayaking
👉 Adventure lovers? Choose Night Kayaking

Still confused? WhatsApp us and we’ll help you choose 😊

Elephant Beach Water Activities

Enjoy the most popular water sports at Elephant Beach, Andaman

Why Elephant Beach?

Elephant Beach is famous for its crystal-clear water, shallow reef, and safe environment for water activities. It is perfect for beginners, families, and adventure lovers.

🤿 Snorkeling

Snorkeling allows you to float on the surface and see colorful fishes and corals. No swimming skills required.

  • Life jacket provided
  • Guide assistance
  • Best for beginners

₹ / person

🪂 Parasailing

Fly high above the sea while being safely attached to a parachute. Enjoy breathtaking aerial views of the island.

  • No experience needed
  • Safety harness & crew
  • Panoramic sea view

₹ / person

🚶‍♂️ Sea Walk

Walk on the sea bed with a special helmet and breathe normally while watching marine life closely.

  • No swimming required
  • Safe oxygen helmet
  • Close coral experience

₹ / person

🚤 Jet Ski Ride

A high-speed water ride for thrill seekers. Ride the waves with full safety support.

  • Instructor guidance
  • Life jacket provided
  • Short & exciting ride

₹ / person

🛟 Sofa Ride

A fun and bumpy inflatable ride pulled by a speed boat. Perfect for friends and families looking for laughter and excitement.

  • Group activity (2–4 persons)
  • Life jacket provided
  • Safe & supervised ride

₹ / person

🚢 Semi Submarine

Enjoy underwater views without getting wet. Sit inside a semi-submerged vessel and watch corals and marine life through large glass windows.

  • No swimming required
  • Air-conditioned cabin
  • Safe for kids & seniors

₹ / person

🤿 Submersible Scooter

Ride an underwater scooter and move freely beneath the sea. A unique experience that feels like flying underwater.

  • Guided underwater ride
  • No swimming skills required
  • Helmet & oxygen supply

₹ / person

🍌 Banana Boat Ride

A fun group ride on an inflatable banana-shaped boat. Perfect for friends and families.

  • Group activity

  • Lots of laughter guaranteed
